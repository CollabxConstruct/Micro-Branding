- Competitive landscape and differentiation

- Voice and tone framework
- Brand architecture models (masterbrand, endorsed, house of brands)
- Competitive positioning tools
- Step-by-step development process
- Best practices and recommended resources

#### 3. **Creative Brief Template** (`templates/Creative-Brief-Template.md`)
Complete creative briefing toolkit (814 lines):
- **Campaign Creative Brief**: Full 13-section brief for campaigns
- **Design Brief**: Project-specific design direction
- **Content Brief**: Blog, social, and content planning
- **Social Media Brief**: Platform-specific social campaigns
- **Video Brief**: Video production planning
- **Writing Guidelines**: Principles for effective briefs
- **Quality Checklist**: Pre-flight verification

#### 4. **Pitch Deck Template** (`templates/Pitch-Deck-Template.md`)
Comprehensive presentation frameworks (916 lines):
- **Client Pitch / New Business Pitch** (18 slides)
- **Capabilities / Credentials Deck** (14 slides)
- **Campaign Strategy Presentation** (15 slides)
- **Case Study Presentation** (12 slides)
- **Internal Strategy Presentation** (13 slides)
- **Design Principles**: Narrative structure, slide design rules
- **Slide-by-Slide Guidelines**: Best practices for each slide type

#### 5. **Content Strategy Template** (`templates/Content-Strategy-Template.md`)
Complete content planning framework (1,235 lines):
- **Content Strategy Overview**: Objectives, mission, audiences
- **Content Pillars Framework**: 5-pillar thematic structure
- **Audience-Content Mapping**: Journey-based content planning
- **Channel Strategy**: Blog, email, social, video, podcast
- **Social Media Strategy**: Platform playbooks (Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Twitter, Facebook)
- **Editorial Calendar**: Planning, workflow, seasonal themes
- **SEO Content Strategy**: Keywords, clusters, optimization
- **Content Audit Framework**: Inventory, assessment, gap analysis
- **Thought Leadership Strategy**: POV, platforms, amplification
- **Content Governance**: Roles, approval processes, tools
- **Measurement & Optimization**: KPIs, reporting, A/B testing

#### 6. **Campaign Strategy Template** (`templates/Campaign-Strategy-Template.md`)
Comprehensive integrated campaign planning framework (1,068 lines):
- **Strategic Foundation**: Business objectives, marketing goals, SMART campaign objectives
- **Audience Strategy**: Deep-dive personas, segmentation, journey mapping (6 stages)
- **Market Context**: Competitive analysis, category insights, consumer insights
- **Creative Platform**: Big idea development, evaluation criteria, key messages
- **Channel & Media Strategy**: Full-funnel strategy, channel selection, media mix, budget allocation
- **Campaign Architecture**: Hero/hub/hygiene framework, amplification (influencer, PR, partnerships)
- **Content Strategy**: Content pillars, mix by format, production planning
- **Go-to-Market Plan**: 5-phase launch (pre-launch, launch, sustain, optimize, extend)
- **Measurement Framework**: KPI hierarchy, attribution models, testing plan, reporting cadence
- **Budget & Resources**: Comprehensive budget breakdown, pacing, team roster
- **Risk Management**: Risk matrix, mitigation strategies, crisis protocol
- **Campaign Calendar**: Master timeline, seasonal planning, week-by-week execution
