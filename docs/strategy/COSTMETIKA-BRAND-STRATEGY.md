**1. Knowledgeable, Not Pretentious**

**4. Elegant, Not Stiff**
- ‚úÖ "A little luxury for your everyday routine"
- ‚ùå "Indulge in opulent self-care rituals"

### Tone Adaptations by Context

| Context | Tone Adjustment | Example |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| **TikTok** | Playful, energetic, trend-aware | "POV: You finally found a foundation that matches üíï #beautytok" |
| **Instagram Feed** | Aspirational, elegant, curated | "Discover the art of effortless beauty. New arrivals in bio." |
| **Instagram Stories** | Casual, behind-the-scenes, authentic | "Let's talk about why this mascara is worth the hype..." |
| **Product Descriptions** | Informative, benefit-focused, helpful | "Buildable coverage that evens tone without feeling heavy. Perfect for normal to oily skin." |
| **Customer Service** | Empathetic, solution-oriented, patient | "I totally understand your concern. Let me help you find the perfect match." |
| **Email Marketing** | Personal, valuable, non-intrusive | "Your favorites are back in stock (and we saved you one)" |

### Writing Guidelines

**Do:**
- Use contractions (we're, you'll, it's)
- Address customers as "you" (second person)
- Lead with benefits, follow with features
- Use inclusive language ("everyone," "all skin tones")
- Inject personality through thoughtful word choice
- Keep sentences short and scannable

**Don't:**
- Use excessive exclamation points (max one per paragraph)
- Use ALL CAPS for emphasis (except acronyms)
- Use gendered assumptions ("ladies," "girls")
- Use jargon without explanation
- Use manipulative urgency tactics ("LAST CHANCE!!!")
- Use tired beauty clich√©s ("slay," "beat face," "glow up" overuse)

## 2.4 Motion & Animation Principles

### Animation Philosophy

**1. Purposeful, Not Decorative**
Every animation communicates meaning:
- **Fade In**: New information arriving
- **Slide Up**: Progressive disclosure
- **Scale In**: Drawing attention
- **Hover Lift**: Interactive affordance

**2. Subtle, Not Distracting**
Animations enhance, never overwhelm:
- Duration: 150-500ms
- Easing: Smooth, natural curves
- Frequency: Intentional, not constant

**3. Delightful, Not Frustrating**
Motion creates joy:
- Product card hover: Slight lift + shadow
- Add to cart: Satisfying microinteraction
- Page transitions: Smooth, seamless
- Loading states: Elegant shimmer

### Standard Motion Durations

- **Fast (150ms)**: Hover states, tooltips, instant feedback
- **Base (300ms)**: Most transitions, reveals, state changes
- **Slow (500ms)**: Page transitions, modals, major reveals

### Motion on Social Platforms

**TikTok**:
- Fast cuts, trend-aligned transitions
- Text reveals synced to audio
- Product showcases with zoom/pan
- Quick-paced tutorials (7-15 seconds per step)

**Instagram Reels**:
- Smooth transitions between products
- Before/after reveals
- Satisfying application sequences
- Aspirational B-roll

**Instagram Stories**:
- Poll stickers, question boxes
- Swipe-up animations
- Product stickers with tap-to-shop
- Behind-the-scenes casual motion

**Guideline**: Use judgment:
- If trend allows brand voice adaptation ‚Üí Yes

## 7.3 Brand Evolution Principles

**When to Evolve:**
- Market shifts require repositioning
- Audience needs change significantly
- Competitive landscape disrupts differentiation
- Business model expands beyond original scope

**When NOT to Change:**
- Short-term trend pressure
- Single piece of negative feedback
- Internal boredom (team fatigue ‚â† customer fatigue)
- Competitor mimicry

**How to Evolve Responsibly:**
- Test changes with small audience segments
- Maintain brand equity (don't throw away recognition)
- Communicate changes transparently to community
- Evolve gradually, not drastically

## Platform-Specific Cheat Sheet

### TikTok
- **Optimal Length**: 15-60 seconds
- **Voice**: Playful, energetic, authentic
- **Format**: Vertical video, captions on, trending audio
- **CTA**: "Link in bio," "Shop now," "Follow for more"

### Instagram Feed
- **Optimal Ratio**: 4:5 (vertical), 1:1 (square)
- **Voice**: Elegant, aspirational, curated
- **Format**: High-quality images, cohesive grid
- **CTA**: "Shop via link in bio," "Tap to shop," "Save for later"

### Instagram Stories
- **Optimal Length**: 7-15 seconds per slide
- **Voice**: Casual, behind-the-scenes, conversational
- **Format**: Vertical, interactive stickers, swipe-ups
- **CTA**: "Swipe up," "Tap to shop," "Reply with..."

### Shopify Blog
- **Optimal Length**: 800-1,500 words
- **Voice**: Informative, helpful, SEO-optimized
- **Format**: Headers, bullets, images, internal links
- **CTA**: "Shop the collection," "Read related post"
