# Part 2: Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

## 2.1 Social Commerce CRO Tactics

### TikTok Shop CRO

**1. Video Hooks Testing**:
- Create 5 versions of same product video with different hooks
- Test: Problem-based, Benefit-based, Curiosity-based, Social proof-based, Trend-based
- Measure: CTR to product page, purchase rate
- Winner: Scale by posting similar hooks

**2. Pricing Tests**:
- Test: $47 vs. $49 vs. $50
- Test: "25% off" vs. "Save $15" vs. "$45 (was $60)"
- Measure: Conversion rate, revenue
- Insight: Charm pricing often wins, but test for your audience

**3. Social Proof Optimization**:
- Add review count to thumbnail: "â˜…â˜…â˜…â˜…â˜… 500+ reviews"
- Feature customer testimonials in video
- Measure: Impact on conversion rate

**4. Urgency Elements**:
- Test: "Limited stock" vs. "Sale ends tonight" vs. No urgency
- Measure: Conversion lift
- Warning: Only use if authentic (false urgency backfires)

---

### Instagram Shopping CRO

**1. Shopping Tag Placement**:
- Test: Tag on product vs. Bottom corner vs. Center of image
- Measure: Tap-through rate to product page
- Best practice: Place where user's eye naturally lands, not covering key visuals

**2. Caption CTA Variations**:
- Test: "Tap to shop" vs. "Link in bio" vs. "Swipe up" vs. "Shop now via link"
- Measure: Link clicks, conversion rate
- Insight: Direct language ("Tap to shop") usually outperforms vague CTAs

**3. Story Sticker Types**:
- Test: Product sticker vs. Link sticker vs. Swipe-up (10K+ followers)
- Measure: Tap-through rate
- Best practice: Product sticker is native, performs better than generic link

**4. Carousel Slide Count**:
- Test: 5 slides vs. 8 slides vs. 10 slides
- Measure: Swipe-through rate, saves, link clicks
- Sweet spot: 7-9 slides (enough value, not overwhelming)

---

### Shopify CRO

**1. Product Page A/B Tests**:
- **Hero Image**: White background vs. Lifestyle shot
- **CTA Button**: "Add to Cart" vs. "Buy Now" vs. "Shop Now"
- **Reviews Position**: Above fold vs. Below description
- **Price Display**: "$49" vs. "$49.00" vs. "$49 (Save $20)"

- Enable **Shop Pay** (1-click checkout for returning customers)

**Discount Ladder** (Frequency from most to least):
1. **Free Shipping**: Most common, low cost to business
2. **Bundle Discounts**: Increases AOV, moves multiple units
3. **First Purchase**: Converts new customers (10-15% off)
4. **Loyalty Rewards**: Retains existing customers (points system)
5. **Flash Sales**: Occasional urgency (15-25% off, 24-72 hours)
6. **Seasonal Sales**: 2-3x per year (20-40% off select items)

**Avoid**:
- âŒ Constant discounting (devalues brand, trains customers to wait for sales)
- âŒ Site-wide sales (hurts margin, not targeted)

**Best Practice**: Discount strategically to achieve specific goals (clear inventory, acquire customers, reward loyalty), not as default strategy.
