## Notes on Execution

1. **Flexibility**: This calendar is a framework, not a rigid rulebook. If a trend emerges or current events warrant adjustment, adapt accordingly.

2. **Quality > Quantity**: If you need to reduce posting frequency to maintain quality, do it. Better to post 15 excellent TikToks per week than 22 mediocre ones.

3. **Community First**: Always prioritize genuine engagement over vanity metrics. Respond to every comment in the first hour of posting.

4. **Authenticity**: If a script doesn't feel natural, rewrite it. Your audience can spot inauthenticity instantly.

5. **Momentum**: The first 30 days are about building habits and systems. By Month 2, content creation should feel streamlined.

6. **Data-Driven Adjustments**: Review metrics weekly and adjust. If educational content consistently outperforms, increase its share.

7. **Sustainability**: This is a marathon, not a sprint. Build a content creation process that you can maintain long-term.
