## Visual Asset Specifications

### TikTok/Instagram Reels
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (vertical)
- **Resolution**: 1080 x 1920 px minimum
- **Format**: MP4
- **Length**: 15-60 seconds (optimal: 30-45 seconds)
- **File Size**: Under 50MB
- **Frame Rate**: 30 fps minimum
- **Text**: Large, readable on mobile; avoid bottom 20% (where captions appear)

### Instagram Feed Posts
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 (square) or 4:5 (portrait)
- **Resolution**: 1080 x 1080 px (square) or 1080 x 1350 px (portrait)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 10MB
- **Text Overlays**: Minimal; caption should contain main message

### Instagram Carousels
- **Slides**: 2-10 images/slides
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 (square) - all slides must be same ratio
- **Resolution**: 1080 x 1080 px per slide
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 10MB per image
- **Design**: Consistent template across all slides

### Instagram Stories
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (vertical)
- **Resolution**: 1080 x 1920 px
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or MP4 (video)
- **File Size**: Under 30MB
- **Safe Zone**: Keep important elements away from edges (150px margin)
- **Text**: Avoid bottom 35% (sticker zone) and top 14% (profile icon)

### Blog Featured Images
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or 2:1
- **Resolution**: 1200 x 630 px minimum (for social sharing)
- **Format**: JPG (optimized for web)
- **File Size**: Under 200KB (for fast loading)
- **Alt Text**: Always include descriptive alt text for SEO and accessibility

### Email Headers
- **Width**: 600px (standard email width)
- **Height**: 200-300px
- **Resolution**: 72 dpi
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 100KB
