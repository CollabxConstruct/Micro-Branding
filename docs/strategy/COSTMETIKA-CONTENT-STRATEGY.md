**Version**: 1.0.0

**Focus Platforms**: TikTok, Instagram (Feed, Stories, Reels)
**Business Model**: Social Commerce (TikTok Shop, Instagram Shopping)

## Executive Summary

COSTMETIKA's content strategy leverages TikTok and Instagram as primary revenue and community channels, transforming social media from brand awareness into direct commerce. This strategy prioritizes education, authenticity, and community engagement over traditional advertising, positioning COSTMETIKA as the beauty brand that teaches before it sells.

**Core Strategy**: Content-led commerce through platform-native formats that entertain, educate, and convert.

**Success Definition**:
- **30-Day Goal**: 10K+ combined followers, 5% engagement rate, 100+ orders from social
- **90-Day Goal**: 75K+ combined followers, 6% engagement rate, $50K+ revenue from social
- **180-Day Goal**: 200K+ combined followers, 7% engagement rate, $150K+ revenue from social

# Part 1: Platform Strategy

## 1.1 TikTok Strategy

### Platform Role
**Primary Function**: Discovery engine, entertainment hub, impulse purchase driver

**Why TikTok Wins for COSTMETIKA**:
- Beauty content thrives (#beautytok has 100B+ views)
- Native shopping through TikTok Shop (seamless checkout)
- Algorithm favors authentic, educational content (our strength)
- Younger demo (18-34) = beauty-forward, digitally native
- Viral potential amplifies reach without paid media

### TikTok Content Pillars Distribution

| Pillar | % of Content | Weekly Posts | Why This Mix |
|--------|--------------|--------------|--------------|
| **Education First** | 35% | 7-8 posts | Algorithm rewards watch time; tutorials = high retention |
| **Community & Connection** | 25% | 5 posts | Duets, stitches, UGC drive engagement + social proof |
| **Inclusive Beauty** | 20% | 4 posts | Diverse representation = broader audience, better sentiment |

**Formats**:
- **Ingredient Breakdown** (15-30s): "What is Hyaluronic Acid?" with simple visuals

**Posting Cadence**: 1-2 daily
**Best Times**: 7-9am, 12-2pm, 7-10pm EST (commute/lunch/evening scroll)

## 1.3 Cross-Platform Content Repurposing

**Maximize Efficiency**: Create once, adapt for each platform

### Content Waterfall Strategy

**1. TikTok First ‚Üí Instagram Reels**
- Post native to TikTok
- Download video (without watermark)
- Repost to Instagram Reels with adjusted caption
- Maintain aspect ratio (9:16 vertical)

**2. Instagram Carousel ‚Üí TikTok Slideshow**
- Create carousel for Instagram Feed
- Export slides as images
- Use TikTok photo mode with trending audio
- Add voiceover or text overlays

**3. Live Shopping ‚Üí Highlight Clips**
- Host live on both platforms simultaneously
- Save replay, clip best moments (product demos, Q&A)
- Post clips as TikToks and Reels
- Add to IG Story Highlights

**4. Blog Post ‚Üí Carousel ‚Üí Video ‚Üí Story**
- Write long-form blog post (1000+ words)
- Extract key points ‚Üí Instagram Carousel (5-10 slides)
- Film short-form video summary ‚Üí TikTok + Reels
- Share snippets ‚Üí Instagram Stories with link sticker

**Repurposing Rules**:
- Adapt caption/copy for platform voice
- Remove watermarks (don't cross-post directly with TikTok logo on IG)
- Adjust hashtags to platform norms
- Optimize aspect ratios (TikTok = 9:16, IG Feed = 4:5 or 1:1)

# Part 4: Influencer & Collaboration Strategy

## 4.1 Influencer Tier System

### Tier 1: Nano-Influencers (1K-10K followers)
**Volume**: 20-30 active per month
**Compensation**: Free product ($50-100 value)
**Expectations**: 1-2 authentic posts/videos reviewing products
**Why**: High engagement rates (5-10%), authentic connection, cost-effective

### Tier 2: Micro-Influencers (10K-100K followers)
**Volume**: 5-10 active per month
**Compensation**: Free product + $100-500 per post
**Expectations**: 2-3 posts across TikTok/IG, Stories series, Reels
**Why**: Niche authority, trusted voice, accessible rates

### Tier 3: Mid-Tier Influencers (100K-500K followers)
**Volume**: 1-2 per quarter
**Compensation**: $1,000-5,000 per campaign
**Expectations**: Multi-post campaign, live shopping appearance, long-term partnership
**Why**: Broader reach, professional content, campaign amplification

### Tier 4: Macro/Celebrity (500K+ followers)
**Volume**: 1-2 per year (special launches)
**Compensation**: $10,000+ or revenue share
**Expectations**: Major campaign, brand ambassadorship, co-creation
**Why**: Mass awareness, credibility, PR value

# Part 5: Performance Measurement

## 5.1 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Awareness Metrics
- **Follower Growth Rate**: Month-over-month % increase
  - **Target**: 15-20% monthly in first 6 months
- **Reach**: Unique accounts reached per post
  - **Target**: 10K+ per post by Month 3
- **Impressions**: Total views across all content
  - **Target**: 100K+ monthly by Month 3

### Engagement Metrics
- **Engagement Rate**: (Likes + Comments + Shares + Saves) / Followers
  - **Target**: 5-7% average (TikTok), 3-5% average (Instagram)
- **Video Completion Rate**: % who watch full video
  - **Target**: 60%+ for TikToks, 50%+ for Reels
- **Saves Rate**: Saves per post (indicates value)
  - **Target**: 5%+ of total engagement

### Conversion Metrics
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: Link clicks / Impressions
  - **Target**: 2-3% on Stories, 1-2% on posts
- **Social Commerce Revenue**: Sales attributed to social channels
  - **Target**: 30% of total revenue from TikTok/IG by Month 6
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**: From social traffic
  - **Target**: $75+
- **Conversion Rate**: Social visitors who purchase
  - **Target**: 2-3%

### Community Metrics
- **UGC Submissions**: Branded hashtag uses per month
  - **Target**: 50+ by Month 3, 200+ by Month 6
- **Comment Sentiment**: % positive vs. negative
  - **Target**: 90%+ positive
- **DM Volume**: Indicates brand interest and engagement
  - **Target**: 50+ daily by Month 6

## 6.4 Crisis Management Protocol

**Potential Issues**:
- Negative viral review
- Product defect/customer complaint goes public
- Influencer partnership backlash
- Copycat accusations
- Controversial trend participation

### Response Framework

**Step 1: Assess (within 1 hour)**
- What happened? Get full context
- Is it valid criticism or misunderstanding?
- What's the sentiment? (Isolated or widespread?)

**Step 2: Respond (within 2-4 hours)**
- Acknowledge publicly if widespread
- Apologize sincerely if at fault
- Provide solution or path forward
- Move detailed resolution to DMs

**Example Response**:
```
"We hear you, and we're so sorry this happened. This isn't the COSTMETIKA
experience we stand for. We're looking into this immediately and will make
it right. Please DM us your order details so we can help directly. üíï"
```

**Step 3: Follow Through**
- Resolve individual cases privately
- If systemic issue: Issue public statement + action plan
- Update community on resolution

**Step 4: Learn & Prevent**
- Post-mortem: What went wrong?
- Update processes to prevent recurrence
- Rebuild trust through transparency
